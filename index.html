My projects

Capitals App

Summary

Soon the school test of knowledge of the capitals of the world and you want to prepare for it? Or maybe you just want to expand your horizons? This app is perfect tool for it. It's very simple - go to site, choose continent and click 'Start' - you're in the game :) After clicking on 'Start' the program randomly selects a country for you from the choosed continent and all you have to do is to write the capital of this country. But the main feature of this app is visual part. The program not only select a country randomly, but also moves the map so that capital of the country is directly in front of your eyes.
Sounds cool, doesn't it?
