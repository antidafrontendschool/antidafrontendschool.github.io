Votzyv

Summary

Oh, I think this app is the largest and grandest of all that I've ever created. It was my first paid peacework job in web. I did this app from September 2017 to December 2017. Have you ever bought anything? Surely you read the reviews before buying a product, isn't it? We live in an era when anyone can write a product review as he wants. Such a review, of course, may not reflect the truth. So my client wanted to solve this problem - he came up with Votzyv. This platform lets entrepreneurs get the most actual and truly reviews of their products. How does it work? Enterpreneur distributes in social networks (Instagram, VK, Facebook, etc.) the link on the site where people who use his products can record a review. These reviews are sent to the enterpreneur and due to them he can communicate with his audience, influence sales, etc.
I think, that's very cool idea, that will help people to make the world better.
